## San Francisco Summary
San Francisco (/ˌsæn frənˈsɪskoʊ/ SAN frən‑SIS‑koh; Spanish for 'Saint Francis'), officially the City and County of San Francisco, is a commercial, financial, and cultural center of Northern California and the United States. The city proper is the fourth most populous in California and 17th most populous in the United States, with 873,965 residents as of 2020.
